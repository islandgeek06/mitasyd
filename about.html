Our Mission

Our mission is to create the next generation of strategic business leaders from underrepresented populations in the technology and startups world. With quantifiable data and analytical skills leveraging programming and database management tools.

The purpose of M.I.T.A.S. Youth Division is to increase the percentage of underrepresented populations in the technology, entrepreneur and business space by offering specialized training in SQL, Python, and advanced business cases, exposure, and mentoring beginning ages 13 - 18 using SQL, Python, and advanced business cases analysis.
